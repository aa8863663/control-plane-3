Key Findings — 25 Models · 21,352 Evaluations · March 2026

No model achieves A+. Post-correction reliability is not demonstrated at scale by any current production system. The benchmark ceiling is grok-3-mini at 90.5%.

GPT-4o scores D — 22.8pp below GPT-4o-mini. Newer flagship models do not outperform predecessors on constraint persistence. Inverse scaling is a consistent pattern.

Claude shows temperature-invariant failure. The entire Claude range clusters at 66–68% with under 2pp variance across temperatures — consistent with architectural constraint suppression, not sampling noise.

DeepSeek-R1 has the smallest control probe degradation — 5.0pp drop — the only model suggesting genuine generalised constraint persistence rather than benchmark-surface familiarity.
