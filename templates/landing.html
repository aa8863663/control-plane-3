No model passes MTCP
The highest grade achieved is {{ best_grade }}. No production LLM reliably maintains constraints across multi-turn correction sequences at scale. {{ total_models }} models tested across 6 providers.
03
Claude shows systematic failure
Claude Sonnet 4 spans only 0.8% across T=0.0–0.8. This is not sampling noise — it is architecture-level systematic constraint failure, consistent regardless of temperature.
04
Different failure modes by architecture
LLaMA 3.1 8B drops 7.1 points from T=0.2 to T=0.8, while Claude varies by less than 2% across all temperatures. Distinct architectural failure modes require different mitigation strategies.
