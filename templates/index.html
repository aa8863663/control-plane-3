The AI Control Gap

Boards are approving AI investments. Regulators are demanding accountability. Auditors are asking for evidence. Most enterprises cannot prove the AI influencing their decisions is under control.

Governance Is Not Enough

Governance defines intent. Evidence demonstrates control. Regulators increasingly require independent verification not frameworks, policies, or vendor assurances.

Independent Assurance

MTCP provides independent empirical evidence that AI systems behave as specified. Not vendor assurance. Independent assurance. Documented. Defensible. Board ready.

{# ── WHAT MTCP DETECTS ─────────────────────────────────────────────────── #}

What MTCP Detects

Constraint Persistence Failure

Models revert to uncorrected behaviour after correction. MTCP detects this through multi-turn interaction probes.

Measured by Boundary Integrity Score

Control Probe Degradation

Performance gaps between primary and concealed control probes indicate potential methodology exposure during training.

Measured by Control Probe Degradation

Language Constraint Failure

Models revert to English under topic pressure when instructed to output in Arabic or another target language. MTCP measures language persistence across correction cycles.

Measured by Language Specification (LANG) vector
