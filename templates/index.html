The Structural Conflict

For twenty years enterprises deployed horizontal capabilities into vertical structures. ERP. Data. Cloud. AI. Every time the same disappointment. Not because the technology failed. Because the operating model was wrong.

Why Governance Is Not Enough

Others sell trust. MTCP sells proof. Others sell declarations. MTCP sells evidence. Governance defines intent. Independent proof demonstrates control.

Independent Proof Infrastructure

Not owned by risk. Not owned by governance. Not owned by technology. Independent of all of them. Because the moment assurance becomes part of the structure it is trying to assure it stops being independent.

What MTCP Detects

Constraint Persistence Failure

Models revert to uncorrected behaviour after correction. MTCP detects this through multi-turn interaction probes.

Measured by Boundary Integrity Score

Control Probe Degradation

Performance gaps between primary and concealed control probes indicate potential methodology exposure during training.

Measured by Control Probe Degradation

Language Constraint Failure

Models revert to English under topic pressure when instructed to output in Arabic or another target language. MTCP measures language persistence across correction cycles.

Measured by Language Specification (LANG) vector
