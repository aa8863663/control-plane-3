Reliability Analysis

This view focuses on performance stability across temperatures. Instead of a dense chart, it surfaces the models that remain consistent and the models that swing most under sampling variation.

What this page shows

Temperature stability is used here as a quick reliability signal. Lower variance suggests more consistent behaviour under changing sampling conditions. Higher variance suggests performance is more sensitive to temperature.
