External Governance Advisory

Independent attestation that MTCP's structural independence criteria are met.

Purpose

The External Governance Advisory provides independent attestation that MTCP's evaluation methodology meets structural independence criteria. Members have no equity, partnership, or commercial dependency relationship with MTCP. They confirm process independence, not result accuracy. They do not endorse commercial products or specific evaluation outcomes.

The advisory exists to strengthen credibility for regulatory engagement and standards body submissions. Independent attestation confirms that MTCP operates without conflicts of interest affecting evaluation outcomes.

Status

The External Governance Advisory is being constituted. Membership criteria require no equity stake, no commercial partnership, no revenue dependency on evaluation outcomes, no employment relationship, and active practice in AI governance or AI safety research. Members are being confirmed.

For enquiries regarding advisory membership or the independence criteria, contact admin@mtcp.live.
