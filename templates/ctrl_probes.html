Control Probe Validation

20 harder edge-case probes used to validate MTCP methodology · T=0.0 · single run per model

What are control probes? Control probes are a set of 20 deliberately harder edge cases designed to stress-test the benchmark methodology. They are not comparable to main benchmark scores (200/252 probes). Lower pass rates here are expected — these probes expose failure modes at the extremes of constraint persistence.

Key Observations

• All models score lower on control probes than on the main benchmark — as expected by design.

• Control probe results confirm the main benchmark is not trivially easy — models that score highly on the main benchmark also tend to score higher on control probes.

• The control probe set is kept private alongside probes_200.json to prevent gaming.

• Control probes will expand to 50 probes in the next dataset version.
