Key Findings
No model achieves Grade A+ (≥95%). Post-correction reliability is not demonstrated at scale by any current production system.
GPT-4o scores 22.8pp below GPT-4o-mini — newer flagship models do not outperform predecessors on constraint persistence.
Claude models show temperature-invariant failure — consistent with architectural constraint suppression.
LLaMA models show temperature-sensitive failure — consistent with stochastic sampling-induced reversion.
DeepSeek-R1 shows the smallest control probe degradation (5.0pp) — only model indicating genuine generalised persistence.
